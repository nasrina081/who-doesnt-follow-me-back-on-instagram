Who doesn't follow me back?

By Nasrin Ali

How to Use:

1. Go to your Instagram profile -> click the three bars (top right)

2. Open Accounts Center

3. Press 'Your information & permissions'

4. Select 'Download your information', but only select 'Some of your info'

5. Only check followers and following, then download to device, and wait

6. Once the file is ready in your email, unzip it

7. Find the followers.html and following.html -> upload them below
